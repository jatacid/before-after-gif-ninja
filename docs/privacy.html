Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 21, 2025

Overview

Before After Gif Ninja is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or the images you capture.

Data Collection

The extension operates entirely locally on your device. When you capture screenshots or generate GIFs:

  • All processing is done within your browser.
  • No images are uploaded to any external servers.
  • No usage analytics or tracking scripts are included.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions to function:

  • activeTab: To capture the visible area of the current tab.
  • storage: To save your preferences locally (e.g., animation speed).

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via our GitHub Repository.
